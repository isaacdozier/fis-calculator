Convert Numbers

Decimal to Inch Fraction

Inch Fraction to Decimal

Convert Expressions

Short Expressions
*add|subtract|multiply|divide

1.50' * 1.50' = 02-09-00
2.00' * 01.06.08 = 03-01-00
02.00.00 * 01.02.04 = 02-04-08

Long Expressions
*add|subtract OR *multiply|divide

1.50' * 2.00' * 2.00'
= 06-00-00

1.50' * 01.06.00 * 2.00'
= 04-06-00

01.06.00 * 02.06.00 * 02.00.00
= 07-06-00
